Family of Celeste Rivas Hernandez Breaks Silence After D4vd Charged With Teen's Murder

Los Angeles, CA — April 23, 2026. The family of Celeste Rivas Hernandez, the teenage girl at the center of a shocking murder case, has released their first public statement following the formal arrest and criminal charges filed against popular music artist D4vd, whose legal name has been identified in court documents filed in Los Angeles County. The statement, brief but emotionally devastating, has resonated with millions of people across the country who have been closely following the unfolding legal proceedings.

"We miss her deeply. All we want is justice for Celeste," the Rivas Hernandez family stated, in words that have since spread across social media platforms and dominated headlines nationwide.

The Charges Filed Against D4vd

D4vd, the Houston-born artist who rose to international fame with his breakthrough single "Romantic Homicide" and subsequent albums, was taken into custody earlier this week and formally charged with first-degree murder in connection with the death of Celeste Rivas Hernandez. Prosecutors allege the crime occurred in the greater Los Angeles area, though specific details surrounding the circumstances of the teenager's death remain under a partial information seal pending further investigation.

Law enforcement officials confirmed that D4vd was booked at a Los Angeles County detention facility. He entered a not guilty plea at his arraignment hearing, according to court records. His legal team has not yet issued a formal statement regarding the charges, though sources close to the defense indicated they intend to vigorously contest the allegations.

The Los Angeles County District Attorney's office confirmed the charges in a brief press release, noting that investigators had worked for several weeks building the case before making an arrest. Bail was set at a substantial amount during the arraignment, and as of the publication of this article, D4vd remained in custody.

Who Was Celeste Rivas Hernandez?

Celeste Rivas Hernandez was a teenager from Southern California described by those who knew her as vibrant, creative, and deeply loved by her family and friends. She was known within her school community for her passion for the arts and her warm personality. Friends remembered her as someone who lit up every room she entered and had a promising future ahead of her.

The circumstances that connected Celeste to the music industry figure at the center of this case have not been fully disclosed by investigators, though authorities have indicated that the investigation remains ongoing and additional details will be released as the case progresses through the legal system.

Those who knew Celeste personally have described the weeks since her death as an unimaginable nightmare for everyone in her orbit, particularly her immediate family, who had been silent in their grief until this week's statement.

The Family's Statement and What It Means

Legal and public relations experts say that a family statement at this early stage of criminal proceedings is a carefully considered decision. By speaking out now, the Rivas Hernandez family signals to the public, the media, and the legal system that they are engaged and expect accountability.

Flowers and photographs have been placed at a makeshift memorial near her family's home.

Shock in the Music Industry

The arrest of D4vd has sent tremors through the music world. The artist had been celebrated as one of the most distinctive voices of his generation, known for blending indie pop, bedroom pop, and alternative sounds in a way that resonated deeply with young listeners around the globe. His music had amassed hundreds of millions of streams, and he had been in the midst of what appeared to be a career-defining period.

Industry colleagues have largely remained silent since the arrest, with most declining to comment publicly given the serious and sensitive nature of the allegations. Several major music platforms and promotional partners have paused planned activities associated with the artist pending the outcome of the legal process.

What Happens Next in the Legal Process

Following the arraignment, the case will move through the standard stages of California criminal proceedings. Both the prosecution and the defense will engage in a discovery process, during which evidence is exchanged between legal teams. Pretrial hearings will be scheduled before any potential trial date is set.

Legal analysts who have reviewed publicly available information suggest that cases of this nature can take anywhere from several months to over a year to reach trial, particularly when the charges are as serious as first-degree murder. The complexity of the evidence gathering, the involvement of investigators from multiple agencies, and the high-profile nature of the defendant all contribute to a timeline that may extend well into 2027.

For the Rivas Hernandez family, the road ahead is long. Navigating a criminal trial while grieving is among the most difficult experiences any family can endure, according to victim advocacy specialists. Counselors and legal advocates have reportedly been in contact with the family to provide support throughout the process.

Community Calls for Justice

Across social media, the hashtag calling for justice for Celeste has trended repeatedly since the arrest was announced. Thousands of people who never knew the teenager personally have expressed grief and solidarity with her family, a reflection of how deeply the case has touched the public consciousness.

Local community leaders and elected officials in the Los Angeles area have also spoken out, calling on the justice system to handle the case with the full weight of the law and to ensure that the Rivas Hernandez family has access to all the support and resources they need during this period.

"All we want is justice for Celeste." Those eight words, simple and sincere, have become a rallying cry for those following this case. As the legal proceedings move forward, the family of Celeste Rivas Hernandez stands at the center of a story that has gripped the nation, their grief profound and their resolve unmistakable.
