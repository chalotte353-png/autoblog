Dancing With the Stars Kicks Off Season 35 with Two Exciting Celebrity Reveals

The iconic ballroom competition series Dancing With the Stars has wasted no time building anticipation for its upcoming Season 35, officially announcing the first two celebrity contestants set to compete for the coveted Mirrorball Trophy. Reality television favorites Maura Higgins and Ciara Miller have been confirmed as the inaugural names on this season's star-studded roster, sending fans into a frenzy of excitement and speculation.

Both women bring unique backgrounds, vibrant personalities, and dedicated fan bases that are sure to energize the competition from the very first episode.

Who Is Maura Higgins?

Maura Higgins is an Irish television personality who first captured global attention as a contestant on the wildly popular dating series Love Island. Known for her quick wit, fierce confidence, and magnetic screen presence, Higgins quickly became a fan favorite and transitioned into a multi-faceted media career following her time on the show.

More recently, Higgins made a significant splash on The Traitors, the psychological strategy competition that has become one of the most talked-about reality programs in recent memory. Her performance on that series demonstrated not only her competitive instincts but also her ability to navigate complex social dynamics under pressure, qualities that could serve her well on the ballroom floor.

Higgins has also built an impressive presence in the fashion and beauty industries, collaborating with major brands and cultivating a loyal following on social media. Her transition to Dancing With the Stars represents yet another chapter in a career that has consistently defied expectations.

Maura Higgins brings an infectious energy and a genuine passion for performance that could make her one of the most compelling competitors this season has ever seen.

While Higgins has no formal dance training on her public resume, her natural charisma and willingness to throw herself wholeheartedly into challenges suggest she could be a dark horse contender for the Mirrorball Trophy.

Who Is Ciara Miller?

Ciara Miller is a nurse and television personality best known for her appearances on Summer House, the Bravo reality series that follows a group of young professionals as they spend their weekends at a house in the Hamptons. Miller joined the cast and quickly became a standout presence, earning praise for her straightforward personality and her ability to bring both drama and warmth to the screen.

Beyond her reality television work, Miller has been open about her dedication to her nursing career, a commitment that has earned her widespread admiration from fans who appreciate her grounded perspective despite her growing fame. Her dual identity as both a healthcare professional and a television personality has made her a relatable and aspirational figure for many viewers.

Miller's competitive spirit, which has been on full display throughout her time on Summer House, will likely translate well to the demands of Dancing With the Stars, where contestants must balance technical learning, emotional vulnerability, and weekly pressure to deliver memorable performances.

What This Means for Season 35

The decision to lead Season 35 announcements with Maura Higgins and Ciara Miller signals a clear intention from the show's producers to attract a diverse and engaged viewership. Both women command passionate fan communities that span multiple demographics and geographic regions, which bodes well for the season's ratings prospects.

Dancing With the Stars has long been celebrated for its ability to bring unexpected celebrity pairings to the ballroom and reveal hidden talents in the most surprising of contestants. Season 35 appears poised to continue that tradition, with two women who are accustomed to performing under pressure and engaging authentically with audiences.

Key Things to Know About the Season 35 Announcement

  • Maura Higgins is known for her appearances on Love Island and The Traitors, as well as her successful brand partnerships and media career.
  • Ciara Miller rose to prominence on Summer House and has maintained a career as a registered nurse alongside her television work.
  • Both celebrities represent the first wave of cast announcements, with additional names expected to be revealed in the coming weeks.
  • The pairing of a transatlantic reality star with an American reality personality reflects the show's ongoing effort to appeal to both domestic and international audiences.
  • Season 35 is expected to premiere later in 2026, though an official date has not yet been confirmed.

Fan Reactions and Early Predictions

Social media erupted almost immediately following the announcements, with fans of both celebrities expressing enthusiasm about seeing their favorites take on the challenge of competitive ballroom dancing. Supporters of Maura Higgins highlighted her previous experiences in high-pressure competitive environments as reasons to believe she could go deep into the competition, while fans of Ciara Miller pointed to her athletic background and natural poise as potential advantages on the dance floor.

Early fan predictions have ranged from cautious optimism to outright declarations that either woman could be the season's champion. What is clear from the reaction across social platforms is that both celebrities have activated their respective fan bases in a meaningful way, which typically translates into strong viewer engagement throughout a season.

The combination of Maura's international appeal and Ciara's devoted American fan base could make Season 35 one of the most socially active seasons in the show's history.

A Season Full of Promise

With only two celebrities announced so far, the full picture of Season 35's cast remains to be seen. However, the early reveals suggest that producers are prioritizing personalities who are not only recognizable but also genuinely capable of delivering the kind of emotional and entertaining moments that keep audiences coming back week after week.

Dancing With the Stars has proven time and again that the magic of the show lies not just in the dancing itself but in the personal journeys that unfold as celebrities push beyond their comfort zones, form meaningful connections with their professional partners, and discover capabilities they never knew they possessed. If the first two confirmed cast members are any indication, Season 35 has every ingredient needed to be a truly memorable chapter in the show's storied history.

As additional cast announcements are made in the weeks ahead, anticipation for the new season will only continue to build. For now, fans of Maura Higgins, Ciara Miller, and Dancing With the Stars have plenty to celebrate and much to look forward to as the ballroom prepares to light up once again.
