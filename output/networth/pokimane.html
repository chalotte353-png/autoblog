About Pokimane

Imane Anys, known online as Pokimane, is a Moroccan-Canadian content creator, streamer, and internet personality. Born on May 14, 1996, in Morocco and raised in Canada, she has become one of the most recognized female streamers in the world, with an estimated net worth of $20 million.

Pokimane began streaming on Twitch in 2013 while studying chemical engineering at McMaster University. Her engaging personality, gaming skills, and authentic interactions with viewers quickly attracted a massive following. She eventually left university to pursue content creation full-time, a decision that proved enormously successful.

Streaming Career

On Twitch, Pokimane has accumulated over 9 million followers, making her one of the platform's most-followed streamers. She primarily streams games like Valorant, League of Legends, and variety content. Her streams consistently attract tens of thousands of concurrent viewers, placing her among the top earners on the platform.

She is also a founding member of OfflineTV, a content creator group that produces collaborative videos and streams. Her YouTube channel has over 7 million subscribers across her main and secondary channels, providing additional revenue through ad monetization.

Business & Brand Deals

Pokimane has secured lucrative partnerships with major brands including Postmates, Midnight Snacks (her own snack brand), and various gaming companies. She signed an exclusive streaming deal with Twitch worth millions of dollars. Her merchandise line and brand collaborations contribute significantly to her overall net worth.

In 2023, she made history as one of the first major streamers to leave Twitch for other platforms, signing with Kick before eventually returning. Her influence on the streaming industry and creator economy has been widely recognized.
