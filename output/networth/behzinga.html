Behzinga: From Gaming Enthusiast to Sidemen Superstar

Ethan Payne, professionally known as Behzinga, is a British YouTuber and content creator who has become one of the most recognizable faces in UK entertainment and gaming culture. Born in 1998, Behzinga rose to prominence as a founding member of the Sidemen, a collective of seven content creators that revolutionized group entertainment on YouTube. With over 20 million subscribers on his main channel as of 2026, Behzinga has established himself as a central figure in modern digital entertainment.

Early Career and Rise to Fame

Behzinga began his YouTube journey creating gaming content, primarily focused on FIFA and Call of Duty gameplay videos. His engaging personality and humorous commentary quickly attracted a dedicated audience. However, it was his involvement with the Sidemen collective that catapulted him to mainstream success. The Sidemen, which includes creators like KSI, Miniminter, and Vikram Barn, became known for their large-scale challenges, pranks, and entertainment content that generated hundreds of millions of views.

Sidemen Success and Collective Impact

As a co-founder of the Sidemen, Behzinga played a crucial role in developing the group's format and content strategy. The collective's videos consistently rank among YouTube's most viewed content, with individual videos regularly surpassing 10-20 million views. The Sidemen's success extended beyond YouTube, spawning a merchandise empire that became a significant revenue stream. Behzinga's contribution to the group's chemistry and comedic timing has made him an integral part of the collective's appeal.
