Behzinga: The Rise of Ethan Payne

Behzinga, the stage name of Ethan Payne, is one of the United Kingdom's most influential content creators and a cornerstone member of the Sidemen, arguably Britain's most successful YouTube collective. As of 2026, with an estimated net worth of approximately $20 million, Behzinga represents a new generation of digital entrepreneurs who have transformed online entertainment into a lucrative business empire.

Early Life and YouTube Beginnings

Ethan Payne was born in 1998 or 1999 in the United Kingdom and developed an early passion for gaming and online entertainment. He began his YouTube journey in the mid-2010s, initially focusing on gaming content, Call of Duty videos, and entertaining commentary that resonated with younger audiences. His charismatic personality, humor, and authenticity quickly set him apart from other gaming creators, earning him a growing subscriber base throughout the mid-2010s.

The Sidemen Era

Behzinga's career trajectory accelerated significantly when he became a core member of the Sidemen around 2014-2015. The Sidemen collective, which includes content creators KSI, Miniminter, Wroetoshaw, Vikkstar123, Callux, Calfreezy, and Tobjizzle, revolutionized YouTube entertainment in the UK and beyond. The group created a unique format of challenge videos, team competitions, and lifestyle content that attracted hundreds of millions of views across their channels.

As part of the Sidemen, Behzinga contributed to some of the group's most iconic videos and series, including the legendary "Sidemen Tinder" series, "Sidemen Blind Dating," "Sidemen Soccer," and numerous high-production challenge videos. These collaborations not only boosted his personal channel but also established him as a fixture in UK internet culture. The Sidemen's collective influence expanded beyond YouTube to include merchandise, live events, and special projects that generated significant revenue streams for all members.
