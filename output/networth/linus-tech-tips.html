Floatplane: A Game-Changing Venture

In 2018, Linus launched Floatplane, a subscription-based streaming platform designed to give creators greater control over their content and revenue. This was a bold move that reflected Linus's understanding of industry trends and the challenges creators faced with YouTube's algorithm-dependent model. Floatplane offered exclusive content, early access to videos, and ad-free viewing for paying subscribers, creating a direct creator-to-fan relationship that bypassed traditional platform intermediaries.

Floatplane's success has been instrumental in diversifying Linus's income streams. By 2026, the platform serves as a significant revenue generator, with thousands of paid subscribers enjoying exclusive content and supporting their favorite creators directly. This venture positioned Linus not just as a content creator but as a platform founder, adding another revenue dimension to his portfolio.
