Who Is Zerkaa? The Story of Josh Bradley

Josh Bradley, better known online as Zerkaa, is one of the most recognisable faces in British YouTube culture. Born on September 4, 1992, in London, England, Josh grew up with a passion for gaming and entertainment that would eventually translate into a thriving digital career. Over more than a decade in the online content creation space, he has built a loyal fanbase, co-founded one of the most successful YouTube collectives in the world, and established himself as a multifaceted creator whose influence extends well beyond gaming.

Early Life and the Road to YouTube

Josh Bradley was raised in London, and like many creators of his generation, he discovered YouTube in his teenage years as both a viewer and a creative outlet. He began uploading gaming videos — primarily focused on titles like FIFA and Call of Duty — under the alias Zerkaa, a name that would become synonymous with authentic, relatable British content. His early videos were rough around the edges, as is the case with most young creators, but his natural charisma and genuine passion for gaming helped him stand out in a crowded space.

In the early 2010s, Josh's channel began gaining traction as he refined his content style and built a community of dedicated viewers. His approachable personality and willingness to engage with his audience helped foster a sense of loyalty that many larger channels struggled to replicate. It was during this formative period that Josh began collaborating with a group of like-minded creators who would go on to change the face of UK YouTube forever.

The Sidemen: A Landmark Collaboration

In 2013, Josh Bradley became one of the founding members of The Sidemen, a collective of seven British YouTubers that also includes KSI, Miniminter (Simon Minter), TBJZL (Tobi Brown), Behzinga (Ethan Payne), Vikkstar123 (Vikram Barn), and W2S (Harry Lewis). The group quickly became a cultural phenomenon in the UK, drawing millions of fans through their collaborative videos, FIFA challenges, and larger-than-life personalities.

The Sidemen's group channel grew to accumulate tens of millions of subscribers, producing viral content including elaborate challenge videos, travel vlogs, and the iconic Sidemen Sunday series. Josh's role within the collective has been integral — while he may not always command the headlines in the same way as some of his louder counterparts, his steady presence, thoughtful contributions, and creative input have been pillars of the group's long-term success.

Sidemen Charity Football Matches

Among the most celebrated Sidemen events are their charity football matches, which have drawn hundreds of thousands of live viewers and raised significant sums for charitable causes. Josh has participated in multiple editions of these matches, which have featured appearances from influencers, footballers, and celebrities from around the world. These events have become landmark moments in the YouTube calendar, showcasing the Sidemen's ability to operate at a truly mainstream entertainment level.

Solo Channel and Content Evolution

Beyond the Sidemen, Josh has continuously developed his solo YouTube presence. His main channel, Zerkaa, covers a wide variety of content including gaming videos, vlogs, reaction content, and personal life updates. Over the years, he has diversified away from purely gaming content to reflect his broader interests and life experiences — including his relationship and eventual marriage to Freya Allen (known online as FreyaPlays), a fellow content creator.

This evolution has allowed Josh to maintain relevance in a constantly shifting digital landscape. As tastes have changed and gaming content has become increasingly competitive, his pivot to more lifestyle and personality-driven content has ensured that he retains a dedicated and engaged audience. His transparency about personal matters, including challenges and milestones in his life, has deepened the connection between himself and his viewers.

Additional Channels and Projects

  • ZerkaaPlays — A secondary channel dedicated to gaming content, allowing Josh to publish more niche and longer gaming videos without disrupting the flow of his main channel.
  • Sidemen collaborations — Regular appearances on the main Sidemen channel, which has over 10 million subscribers and continues to be one of the top-performing channels in the UK.
  • Podcast and panel appearances — Josh has appeared on various YouTube podcasts and collaborative projects, broadening his exposure across different audiences.

Business Ventures and Revenue Streams

Like many creators at his level, Josh Bradley's income is not limited to ad revenue alone. A significant portion of his estimated net worth derives from the Sidemen Clothing brand, a merchandise line that has grown into a fully-fledged fashion operation with multiple product drops, limited editions, and dedicated storefronts. The brand's success has been remarkable, reflecting the purchasing power and loyalty of the Sidemen fanbase.

Sponsorships and brand partnerships have also been a consistent income stream. Josh has worked with gaming-focused brands such as GFuel, as well as broader consumer brands seeking access to the lucrative 18–35 male demographic that makes up a large portion of his audience. These deals, while not always publicly disclosed in full, represent a meaningful contribution to his overall earnings.

Net Worth Estimate and Financial Standing

Based on publicly available data, industry estimates, and analysis of his multiple income streams, Zerkaa's net worth is estimated to be in the region of $4 million. This figure accounts for his YouTube ad revenue across both his main and secondary channels, his share of Sidemen merchandise and brand revenues, ongoing sponsorship income, and the accumulated value of his digital platforms. While he may not be the wealthiest member of the Sidemen collective — KSI and Miniminter are generally considered to have higher individual net worths — Josh's financial position is comfortably strong and continues to grow.

Personal Life

Josh Bradley is married to Freya Allen, known online as FreyaPlays, who is herself a content creator with a substantial following. The couple have been open about their relationship on social media and in their respective content, and their dynamic has endeared them to fans of both creators. Their shared life in London has been documented in vlogs and personal updates, giving fans a genuine window into their world.

Legacy and Influence

Josh Bradley's legacy in British YouTube culture is already well-established. As a co-founder of the Sidemen, he helped create a template for collaborative content creation that influenced an entire generation of British creators. His longevity in a notoriously fickle industry is a testament to his adaptability, authenticity, and genuine connection with his audience. As the Sidemen continue to expand their brand into events, media, and beyond, Josh's place in that story remains an important one. Whether through gaming videos, lifestyle vlogs, or viral group challenges, Zerkaa continues to be a name that resonates deeply with millions of fans across the UK and around the world.
