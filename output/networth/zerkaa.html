Zerkaa: The Rise of Josh Bradley

Joshua Bradley, professionally known as Zerkaa, is a British YouTuber, entrepreneur, and one of the founding members of the Sidemen, arguably the most successful YouTube collective in the United Kingdom. Born in 1997, Zerkaa has evolved from a competitive gamer into a mainstream entertainment content creator, with an estimated net worth of approximately $25 million as of 2026.

Early Career and Gaming Background

Zerkaa's journey into content creation began in the early 2010s when he was deeply involved in competitive gaming, particularly Call of Duty. His skills and personality made him stand out in the gaming community, and he began building an audience through his gameplay videos and commentary. Unlike many gaming creators who remained niche, Zerkaa recognized the potential for broader entertainment content and began transitioning his channel accordingly.

The Sidemen Revolution

The defining moment of Zerkaa's career came with the formation of the Sidemen in 2013. Co-founded with fellow creators KSI, Miniminter, Behzinga, Vikram Barn, W2S, and Callux, the Sidemen became a cultural phenomenon in YouTube entertainment. The collective's format—mixing gaming, challenges, pranks, and lifestyle content—resonated with millions of viewers worldwide. What set the Sidemen apart was their chemistry, production quality, and ability to create content that appealed beyond traditional gaming audiences.

Zerkaa's role within the Sidemen proved crucial to their success. His strategic thinking, creative input, and ability to engage audiences made him one of the group's most valuable members. The Sidemen's main channel grew to over 20 million subscribers, with individual members like Zerkaa maintaining substantial independent followings. The collective has generated billions of views, translating into significant revenue streams for all members.

Merchandise and Business Ventures

Recognizing the business potential of their brand, Zerkaa and the Sidemen launched an official merchandise line that became wildly successful. The Sidemen merch drops regularly sell out within hours, generating millions of pounds in annual revenue. Zerkaa's entrepreneurial mindset extended beyond merchandise to various business investments and ventures, diversifying his income sources and securing long-term wealth.

Content Evolution and Diversification

Throughout the 2010s and into the 2020s, Zerkaa adapted his content strategy to changing platform dynamics and audience preferences. While the Sidemen remained central to his brand, he also maintained an active personal YouTube channel with millions of subscribers. His content evolved from pure gaming to include challenge videos, vlogs, philanthropic content, and entertainment pieces that showcased his personality beyond gaming skills.

The creator has leveraged TikTok and Instagram to reach younger audiences, recognizing the importance of platform diversification. Short-form content from his videos consistently goes viral, introducing his content to millions of new viewers regularly. This multi-platform approach has been instrumental in maintaining relevance in the rapidly evolving creator economy.

Revenue Streams and Wealth Accumulation

By 2026, Zerkaa's net worth reflects the cumulative success of his various revenue streams. His primary income comes from YouTube ad revenue across his personal channel and the Sidemen collective channels. Influence and Legacy

Beyond financial metrics, Zerkaa's influence on YouTube culture and digital entertainment is substantial.
