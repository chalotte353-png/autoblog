Zerkaa: From Gaming Enthusiast to Sidemen Mogul

Josh Bradley, professionally known as Zerkaa, stands as one of the most influential content creators in the United Kingdom and a pivotal member of the globally recognized Sidemen collective. With an estimated net worth of $20-25 million as of 2026, Zerkaa represents the evolution of YouTube gaming content from a niche hobby into a multi-million pound entertainment enterprise.

Early Career and YouTube Foundation

Zerkaa's journey in content creation began in the early 2010s when he started uploading gaming videos to YouTube. Unlike many creators who focused on a single game, Josh differentiated himself through his charismatic personality, sharp sense of humor, and versatility across multiple gaming titles. His early content primarily centered around FIFA gameplay, where he showcased impressive skills and entertaining commentary that resonated with the gaming community.

The turning point in his career came with the formation of the Sidemen in 2013. Recognizing the potential of collaborative content, Zerkaa joined forces with fellow content creators including KSI, Miniminter, Behzinga, Vikk, Tobi, and JJ to create the Sidemen collective. This strategic move transformed from individual YouTube channels into a powerhouse collaborative brand that would eventually dominate the UK YouTube landscape.

The Sidemen Empire and Content Evolution

The Sidemen's approach to content creation was revolutionary for its time. Rather than competing individually, the collective pooled their audiences and resources to create high-production-value videos featuring challenges, collaborations, and entertainment that transcended traditional gaming content. Zerkaa's contributions to the Sidemen were instrumental in their success, bringing both technical gaming knowledge and entertainment value to their productions.

By the mid-2020s, the Sidemen had become a cultural phenomenon in the UK and beyond. Their YouTube channel accumulated billions of views, and individual members like Zerkaa built substantial independent followings. Zerkaa's personal YouTube channel grew to over 5 million subscribers, with his content diversifying beyond FIFA to include reaction videos, collaboration content, and entertainment challenges that appealed to a broader audience.
