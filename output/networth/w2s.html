📖 Full Biography

Who Is W2S (Harry Lewis)?

Harry Lewis, widely known by his online alias W2S (an abbreviation of 'Wroetoshaw'), is one of the most recognizable and beloved figures in British YouTube culture. Born on November 24, 1996, in Guernsey, a small island off the coast of Normandy, Harry grew up with a passion for football and video games that would eventually catapult him to international internet fame. Over the course of a decade-long career, he has built a media empire that spans YouTube, social media, live events, and merchandise, establishing himself as a multi-millionaire and a household name among fans of online content.

Early Life and Background

Harry Lewis spent his formative years on the island of Guernsey, where life was relatively quiet and community-oriented. From a young age, he demonstrated a natural affinity for football — a passion that would become the cornerstone of his content. His family, including his older brother Callux (also a prominent YouTuber), played a significant role in his early interest in online content creation. Watching his brother build an audience online gave Harry the blueprint and the encouragement he needed to try it himself.

Harry began uploading videos to YouTube in his early teenage years, initially focusing on FIFA — the wildly popular football simulation video game series by EA Sports. His raw energy, comedic commentary, and relatable personality quickly attracted a young audience hungry for entertaining gaming content. What started as a hobby soon became something far larger than he could have imagined.

Rise to YouTube Stardom

The W2S channel grew rapidly through the early 2010s as the FIFA gaming community exploded in popularity. Harry's content — centered around FIFA Ultimate Team pack openings, skill challenges, and hilarious commentary — resonated deeply with a generation of teenagers who shared his love of the game. His self-deprecating humor, infectious laugh, and willingness to embarrass himself on camera made him instantly likable and highly shareable.

By his mid-teens, Harry had amassed millions of subscribers, cementing his place as one of the UK's leading gaming YouTubers. His growth was further accelerated by his association with the Sidemen, a collective of seven prominent British YouTubers that also includes KSI, Miniminter (Simon Minter), Zerkaa (Josh Bradley), Behzinga (Ethan Payne), Vikkstar123 (Vikram Barn), and TBJZL (Tobi Brown).

The Sidemen: A Collective Force

Harry's involvement with the Sidemen has been one of the most significant factors in his continued success and wealth accumulation. The Sidemen are not merely a group of friends who make videos together — they are a fully-fledged media brand with their own clothing line, YouTube channel, charity events, and cultural influence that extends well beyond the internet.

The Sidemen YouTube channel, which features group challenges, holiday videos, and collaborative content, has surpassed 20 million subscribers and generates tens of millions of views per video. Harry is a central and beloved figure within the group, known for his boisterous personality, willingness to take on absurd challenges, and his genuine camaraderie with his fellow members.

  • Sidemen Charity Matches: Perhaps the Sidemen's most high-profile ventures are their annual charity football matches, which pit the Sidemen against teams of fellow YouTubers and celebrities. These events have sold out large UK stadiums, attracted hundreds of thousands of live stream viewers, and raised significant sums for charitable causes. Harry, as a lifelong football lover, has been particularly prominent in these events.
  • SDMN Clothing: The Sidemen merchandise brand has been a consistent revenue driver, selling branded clothing and lifestyle products to their enormous fanbase around the world.
  • Sidemen Sunday: The group's consistent weekly uploads on the Sidemen channel, commonly referred to as 'Sidemen Sunday,' have become a cultural institution for millions of young viewers in the UK and globally.

Content Evolution and Personal Brand

While Harry built his reputation on FIFA content, his channel has evolved significantly over the years. As his audience matured, so did his content — expanding into vlogs, challenge videos, lifestyle content, and reaction videos. This evolution has allowed him to retain his core audience while attracting new viewers who may not be exclusively interested in gaming.

Harry has also demonstrated a willingness to be vulnerable and authentic with his audience, discussing personal struggles and mental health in an era where such conversations are increasingly valued. This authenticity has deepened his connection with fans and reinforced his position as more than just a gaming commentator — he is a personality.

Net Worth and Income Breakdown

As of 2024, Harry Lewis's estimated net worth is approximately $20 million, though some estimates place this figure higher depending on the valuation of his stake in Sidemen-related ventures. His wealth has been accumulated through multiple streams:

  • YouTube Ad Revenue: With over 16 million subscribers on his main channel and billions of cumulative views, Harry's YouTube ad revenue alone is estimated to generate several hundred thousand dollars annually.
  • Sidemen Merchandise: As a co-owner and prominent member of the Sidemen brand, Harry benefits from the group's highly successful clothing and merchandise operation.
  • Brand Sponsorships: Harry has secured deals with major brands including EA Sports, G-Fuel, and various other companies looking to reach his young, engaged demographic.
  • Event Revenue: The Sidemen charity matches and related events generate ticket sales, pay-per-view revenue, and sponsorship income that is distributed among the group's members.

Personal Life

Harry Lewis is known for keeping aspects of his personal life relatively private despite his enormous public profile. He has been open about living in London, where much of the Sidemen content is filmed and produced. His relationship with his fellow Sidemen members is clearly one of genuine friendship that extends beyond professional collaboration, a dynamic that is evident in their content and one that fans find deeply appealing.

Legacy and Influence

W2S represents a generation of British YouTube creators who turned a passion for gaming and entertainment into legitimate, multi-million dollar careers. His journey from a teenager in Guernsey uploading FIFA videos to a globally recognized internet personality is a testament to the democratizing power of online platforms. As YouTube and digital media continue to evolve, Harry Lewis remains a relevant, energetic, and beloved figure whose influence on British internet culture is undeniable.
