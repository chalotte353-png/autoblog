Dixie grew up in Norwalk, Connecticut, in a creative family that would eventually shape the entertainment landscape. Her younger sister Charli also became a prominent TikToker, creating a family brand that expanded across multiple platforms. Dixie initially gained attention through her dancing and lip-syncing videos on TikTok, where her charisma, dance skills, and consistent content creation earned her a rapidly growing following.

By 2020, Dixie had amassed millions of followers on TikTok, establishing herself as one of the platform's most prominent creators. Her content, characterized by trendy dances, comedic skits, and relatable teenage humor, resonated with Gen Z audiences worldwide.
