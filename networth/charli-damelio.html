About Charli D'Amelio

Charli Grace D'Amelio stands as one of the most influential content creators in modern history. Born on May 1, 2004, in Norwalk, Connecticut, Charli rose from relative obscurity to global superstardom in months, fundamentally transforming how entertainment, celebrity, and influence are defined in the digital age. With over 150 million TikTok followers she is the platform's most-followed creator.

Charli's journey began in 2016 when she started posting dance videos online. Trained as a competitive dancer since childhood, her natural talent and ability to master trending dances quickly caught attention. By 2019, she was one of TikTok's top creators. In November 2020, she became the first person ever to reach 100 million followers on TikTok — a milestone that underscored her unprecedented influence.

Cultural Impact

Time Magazine named her one of the Most Influential Teens in both 2019 and 2020. In 2021, Hulu released 'The D'Amelio Show,' a reality documentary series providing an intimate look into Charli's life. The show was critically acclaimed and commercially successful, solidifying her status as an entertainment figure beyond social media.

Financial Success

Charli's net worth of approximately $20 million is derived from multiple revenue streams. Her primary income includes TikTok brand deals, YouTube ad revenue from 26M+ subscribers, and the Hulu series. Brand endorsements with Dunkin' Donuts, Puma, Morphe, and others have been particularly lucrative, often involving multi-year contracts worth millions.

Family & Future

The D'Amelio family operates as a unified entertainment brand. Her sister Dixie D'Amelio is also a successful creator. At just 21, Charli is transitioning into traditional entertainment — acting, hosting, and production — while maintaining her massive social media presence. Her ability to adapt and maintain relevance suggests her influence will endure for years to come.
