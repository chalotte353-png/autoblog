About Jeffree Star

Jeffrey Lynn Steininger Jr., known professionally as Jeffree Star, is an American beauty YouTuber, makeup artist, entrepreneur, and musician. Born on November 15, 1985, in Orange County, California, he has built one of the most successful beauty empires in the world, with an estimated net worth of $200 million.

Jeffree Star began his career on MySpace in the mid-2000s, where he gained a massive following through his music and outrageous personality. He released several music singles and toured extensively before pivoting his career toward the beauty industry. In 2014, he launched Jeffree Star Cosmetics, which would become the cornerstone of his business empire.

Beauty Empire

Jeffree Star Cosmetics quickly became a multi-million dollar brand known for its highly pigmented eyeshadow palettes, liquid lipsticks, and highlighters. His products regularly sell out within minutes of launch, generating tens of millions of dollars in revenue annually. The brand is sold through his own website and major retailers.

His YouTube channel, which has amassed over 15 million subscribers, features beauty tutorials, product reviews, and vlogs. His brutally honest reviews and dramatic personal style have kept him at the forefront of the beauty community for over a decade.

Business Ventures

Beyond cosmetics, Jeffree Star has diversified his income through various business ventures. He owns a large property in Wyoming where he runs a yak farm, which has become a popular subject on his social media channels. He has also invested in real estate and various luxury assets.

His merchandise lines, brand collaborations, and limited-edition collections consistently generate significant revenue. Despite controversies throughout his career, his business acumen has allowed him to maintain and grow his wealth substantially.
