About Miniminter

Who Is Miniminter? The Rise of Simon Minter

Simon Minter, better known by his online alias Miniminter, is one of the most recognisable faces in British YouTube history. Born on September 7, 1992, in Teesside, England, Simon grew up with a passion for football and video games — two interests that would ultimately shape one of the most successful content creator careers the UK has ever seen. From humble beginnings uploading FIFA gameplay videos to his bedroom, Miniminter has grown into a multi-millionaire entrepreneur, collaborator, and internet personality with a global fanbase.

Early Life and the Beginning of a YouTube Career

Simon attended school in the northeast of England before moving to London to pursue his dream of becoming a professional content creator. His early videos were focused almost entirely on FIFA Ultimate Team, the wildly popular game mode within EA Sports' FIFA franchise. His approachable personality, sharp commentary, and genuine love for football resonated with viewers almost immediately. By 2012 and 2013, his channel was gaining serious traction, and it wasn't long before he caught the attention of like-minded creators.

What set Simon apart in those early days was his authenticity. Unlike many creators who adopted over-the-top personas, Miniminter was relatable and grounded — a regular lad from the north of England who happened to be genuinely entertaining on camera. Fans gravitated toward his dry wit, honest reactions, and consistent upload schedule.

The Sidemen: Building an Empire Together

In 2013, Simon became one of the founding members of the Sidemen, a British YouTube supergroup that would go on to become one of the most influential content collectives in internet history. Alongside KSI (Olajide Olatunji), Vikstar123, Wroetoshaw (W2S), Zerkaa, TBJZL, and Behzinga, Simon helped create a group whose combined influence has reached tens of millions of fans across every corner of the globe.

The Sidemen's content ranged from FIFA challenges and real-life stunts to luxury challenges, travel vlogs, and massive group events. The chemistry between the members — full of banter, competitiveness, and genuine friendship — made them appointment viewing for a generation of young viewers in the UK and beyond. Simon's role within the group was often that of the reliable, skilled competitor, regularly performing well in football challenges and FIFA-based content that aligned perfectly with his personal brand.

  • Sidemen YouTube Channel: The group's main channel has surpassed 20 million subscribers and counts billions of total views.
  • MoreSidemen: A secondary channel for more casual, behind-the-scenes content that also commands millions of subscribers.
  • Sidemen Clothing: A successful merchandise line that has evolved into a legitimate fashion brand with dedicated drops and significant revenue.

Personal YouTube Channel and Content Evolution

While the Sidemen remained a cornerstone of his career, Simon also nurtured his personal channel, Miniminter, which has grown to over 10 million subscribers. His solo content evolved from pure FIFA gameplay to include vlogs, Q&As, challenge videos, and reaction content. He has also collaborated extensively with creators outside the Sidemen circle, broadening his audience and keeping his content fresh.

Simon's personal channel is notable for its consistency and longevity. In an era where many YouTubers burn out or fade from relevance, Miniminter has maintained a loyal, engaged audience for over a decade — a remarkable achievement in the fast-moving world of digital content creation.

The Sidemen Charity Match: A Cultural Phenomenon

One of the most celebrated achievements associated with Miniminter and the Sidemen is the annual Sidemen Charity Football Match. These events, held at professional football stadiums including Stamford Bridge (home of Chelsea FC) and the London Stadium, have sold out tens of thousands of seats and attracted massive online viewership. The matches pit the Sidemen against other creators and celebrities, creating spectacular football entertainment while raising significant sums for charitable causes.

The 2022 and 2023 editions in particular became viral global events, drawing in over 2 million concurrent live viewers online and generating widespread press coverage. For Simon, whose love of football has always been at the core of his identity, these events represent the perfect intersection of his passions and his professional reach.

Net Worth and Income Breakdown

Miniminter's estimated net worth in 2024 sits at approximately $20 million, though some estimates place it higher when factoring in property investments and business equity. His wealth is derived from several streams:

  • YouTube Ad Revenue: With tens of millions of views per month across his personal channel and Sidemen content, ad revenue alone generates a substantial annual income, estimated in the hundreds of thousands of dollars per year.
  • Brand Deals and Sponsorships: Simon has partnered with major brands including EA Sports, G Fuel, and various gaming and lifestyle companies. These deals are a major income source, with individual campaigns potentially worth six figures.
  • Sidemen Merchandise and Business: As a co-founder of the Sidemen brand, Simon holds equity in the collective's business operations, including merchandise sales, event revenues, and media licensing.
  • Investments and Property: Like many successful digital creators of his generation, Simon has invested in property in London, adding tangible assets to his overall net worth.

Personal Life and Public Persona

Simon is known for being relatively private compared to some of his Sidemen peers. He has been in a long-term relationship and occasionally shares personal milestones with his audience, though he tends to keep the details of his private life away from the public eye. This balance between openness and privacy has earned him respect from fans who appreciate that not everything needs to be content.

His love of football — particularly his support for Middlesbrough FC, reflecting his northeast England roots — is a recurring theme in his content and public appearances. He has participated in numerous celebrity football events and is widely regarded as one of the more technically capable footballers among the YouTuber community.

Legacy and Influence

More than a decade into his career, Miniminter's legacy is secure. He is a pioneer of British YouTube culture, a key architect of the Sidemen's global success, and a consistent, professional creator who has navigated the volatile landscape of online media with skill and resilience. His influence on a generation of aspiring content creators — particularly in the UK — cannot be overstated.

As the creator economy continues to evolve, Simon Minter appears well-positioned to remain a relevant and financially successful figure for years to come, whether through YouTube, live events, or new business ventures that leverage his massive platform and trusted personal brand.
