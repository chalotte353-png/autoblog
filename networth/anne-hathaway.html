About Anne Hathaway

Anne Hathaway: From Princess Diaries to Oscar Glory

Anne Jacqueline Hathaway, born November 12, 1982, in Brooklyn, New York, has evolved from teen movie star to one of Hollywood's most acclaimed dramatic actresses with an estimated net worth of $80 million in 2026.

Early Career

Hathaway gained widespread recognition with The Princess Diaries (2001), the Disney comedy that introduced her to a global audience. The film spawned a sequel and established her as a bankable leading actress.

The Devil Wears Prada

The Devil Wears Prada (2006) marked a turning point. Starring opposite Meryl Streep, Hathaway delivered a nuanced performance in one of the most beloved fashion films ever made, grossing $327 million worldwide.

Oscar Win

Her portrayal of Fantine in Les Misérables (2012) — performing live vocals and undergoing dramatic physical transformation — earned her the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress. She also played Catwoman in The Dark Knight Rises and starred in Christopher Nolan's Interstellar (2014).
